Game controllers

GameTrak
  • Wikipedia
  • cost: ~$25 // ebay
  • type: 2x 3D joystick
  • connection: USB (HID)
  • examples:
    • lanterns by britta joy peterson + garrett laroy johnson
    • lanterns by garrett laroy johnson
    • MUSICAL APPLICATIONS AND DESIGN TECHNIQUES FOR THE GAMETRAK TETHERED SPATIAL POSITION CONTROLLER by Freed et al.
    • Ensemble, Entrainment, and Movement in the Mess of the Matter: Non-anthropocentric Design of Responsive-Media Environments by Johnson et al
  • DIY possible? Yes, but cheap cost of GameTrak makes DIY unappealing
WiiMote
  • cost: ~$15 / ebay
  • type: 6-dof IMU + buttons + joystick
  • connection: bluetooth via osculator
Console Controller
  • Wikipedia
  • cost: ~$25-60 // ebay
  • type: 2d joystick(s) + buttons (+haptics?)
  • connection: USB (HID) or bluetooth
  • examples:
    • mini savior opt. by lauren sarah hayes
    • giant joystick by mary flanagan
  • DIY possible? Yes, but cheap cost of item makes DIY unappealing
