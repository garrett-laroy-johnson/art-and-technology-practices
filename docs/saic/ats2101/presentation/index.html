SAIC | ATS 2101 | Art and Technology Practices

Group Presentations

Presentation Criteria

Overall

You and 2-3 of your classmates will prepare a 20-30 minute presentation on an area of art and technology studies. Your presentation should cover the items below.

Readings
  • In 1-3 sentences, define the author’s argument or intention.
  • Pull 3 key quotations from the text.
  • Pull and define key terms. Discuss their relevance to the text.
  • Generate a list of 2-3 central questions we as makers this text helps us to ask (e.g.: “How do paradigms of the digital and analog extend into art practice beyond computation?”)
Artists
  • For each artist, present 1 or 2 artworks
  • Give us a sense of how the artworks connect with the readings (e.g. terms, questions, quotations)
Your own connections

How do your interests intersect with this topic / technique? How might you continue to pursue this at this school or in your future work?
