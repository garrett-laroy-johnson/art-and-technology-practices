Wave

Khushi Shah

Fall 2022

Wave is an audio/ music visualizer which acts like a generative sound artwork which people can experience as a piece of art on a wall. Audio Visualizer as the name suggests works by extracting frequency information from an audio and later creates animated structures which depict the waveforms, rhythm, loudness, and frequency of the audio which is being fed in. Audio Visualizer is right now being used as a music visualizer which is the interpretation of digital electronic signals of an audio into animated images. Wave is a music visualizer which people can look at and it can also be used on personal computers. Wave is a music visualizer in which an audio file is loaded and it later synthesis the audio and later all the data is collected about the audio like its wavelength, pitch, etc. and at the end an animated imagery which moves and synchronizes according to the tune of the audio is being formed. So basically, the data of the audio is being converted to an animated image. The use of music visualizers is trending right now especially by DJs in live clubs, people use it as a wallpaper and as a generative art piece.
